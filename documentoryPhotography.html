Documentory Photography

The documentory project Kayla did for her photography class was suppose to be about how her whole town is being over run by the construction of new housing developments. But when she started taking photos she thought they were boring and uninteresting to look at, but had also taken some photes of an old shed up the street from where she lived. She loved the photo and decided to do the project on old abandoned building. She had found that it was much easier to find these places living in such an old farming town. Most of the photos taken were in Dartmouth, New Bedford, and Westport. After taking all of the photos and looking through them she realized that all of the photos have something in common, that she was looking through a portal in every photo. She looks though a camara lense to take the photos then in the photos themselves they all have a doorway, or window, or an archway to look through.
